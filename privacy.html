Privacy Policy

This is a personal, single-user analytics tool used by its owner to review the performance of their own social media videos.

What it accesses

With the owner's explicit authorization, the tool reads public statistics for the owner's own TikTok videos (view, like, comment, and share counts) via the TikTok Display API. It does not access any other user's data.

How data is used and stored

Retrieved statistics are stored locally on the owner's own computer for personal analysis. No data is sold, shared with third parties, or published. Access tokens are stored locally and can be revoked at any time from the owner's TikTok account settings.

Contact

For any questions, contact the app owner directly.
